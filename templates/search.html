Frequently Asked Questions

Use the search bar on the main page to enter the name of the station and view results.

Click on the station name from the search results to see the departure details.

Visit the About Us section in the menu for more information.

Enter the name of the station in the input field and specify the maximum number of results you want to see. Click the "Search" button to submit your query and view the results.

The search form will display a list of stations that match your query along with their departure details. The results will be based on the name of the station and the maximum number of results you specified.

If no results are found, ensure that you have entered the correct station name and try again. If the issue persists, the station might not be available in our database.
